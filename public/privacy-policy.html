Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

1. Local Processing Architecture

Tools24 operates on a unique "Client-Side Only" model. Unlike traditional tools, your data—including images, PDF files, and sensitive UPI information—is processed entirely within your web browser. No files are ever uploaded or transmitted to our servers.

2. Data Collection & Storage

We do not maintain a backend database for user content. Once you close your browser session, all temporary data used during the tool's execution is automatically purged from your device's memory. We do not collect personal identifying information (PII).

3. External Security

Because your data never leaves your device, it remains safe from server-side breaches or unauthorized access. We utilize industry-standard encryption protocols (HTTPS) to deliver our website code securely to your browser.
